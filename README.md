AtliQ Bank Credit Card Analysis
Project Overview

This project analyzes customer behavior and evaluates the effectiveness of a newly launched credit card campaign through Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) and A/B Testing.

The objective is to identify customer segments, generate business insights, and determine whether the new credit card significantly increases customer transaction amounts.

Business Problem

AtliQ Bank plans to launch a new credit card and wants to:

Identify high-potential customer segments
Understand customer demographics
Analyze spending behavior
Measure the effectiveness of the new card using A/B Testing
